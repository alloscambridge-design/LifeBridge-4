About Us

LifeBridge Church was founded on 1st February 2022 with one mission: bridging lives to Jesus Christ. We are a community of believers passionate about connecting people to Christ and to each other. We are committed to worship, discipleship, and serving our community with love and excellence.

🎯 Our Vision

Soul winning and church planting. To reach the lost and establish Christ-centered churches that transform communities.

📖 Our Belief

We believe in the Bible as God's inspired Word and our guideline for faith, life, and doctrine. Christ is our foundation and the Holy Spirit empowers us daily.

🔥 Our Identity

A vibrant youthful church filled with passion, worship, and purpose. Every generation welcome - young at heart, strong in faith.

Our Pastor

Pastor Mukwaya Godfrey

Pastor Mukwaya Godfrey leads LifeBridge Church with a heart for God and people. His vision is to see every soul connected to Christ and living in purpose.
