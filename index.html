LifeBridge Christian School Project

Building Godly Leaders Through Education

Submitted By:
Pastor Godfrey Mukwaya
LifeBridge Church
Kajjansi, Wakiso District, Uganda

1. Executive Summary

LifeBridge Church seeks financial support and partnership to establish LifeBridge Christian School in Kajjansi, Wakiso District, Uganda. The project aims to provide quality, affordable, and Christ-centered education to children from low-income and middle-income families while nurturing future leaders with strong moral and spiritual values.

The church currently owns approximately 58 decimals of land designated for this project. The proposed school will begin with Nursery and Primary sections and expand gradually according to available resources and community demand.

We invite individuals, churches, businesses, and Ugandans in the diaspora to partner with us in transforming lives through education and the Gospel.

2. Background

LifeBridge Church was founded in 2022 by Pastor Godfrey Mukwaya. The church has grown to approximately 100 registered members and is actively involved in community outreach, discipleship, prayer ministry, and support for vulnerable children and families.

The community around Kajjansi continues to experience a growing demand for affordable and quality education. Many children lack access to schools that provide both academic excellence and Christian values.

3. Problem Statement

Many families in our community struggle to afford quality education. Existing schools are either overcrowded, expensive, or lack strong moral and spiritual foundations.

The need for a Christian school that combines academic excellence with character development has become increasingly urgent.

4. Project Goal

To establish a sustainable Christian school that provides quality education and spiritual development for children in Kajjansi and surrounding communities.

5. Project Objectives

  1. Construct classrooms and administrative facilities
  2. Establish a Nursery and Primary School
  3. Provide affordable education for local families
  4. Create employment opportunities for teachers and support staff
  5. Promote Christian values and leadership among children

6. Project Location

Kajjansi, Wakiso District, Uganda
Land Available: 58 Decimals - Church-Owned Land

7. Project Activities

Phase 1

  • Site preparation
  • Fencing and security improvements
  • Construction of initial classroom block
  • Procurement of desks and learning materials

Phase 2

  • Construction of additional classrooms
  • Administration office
  • Staff facilities
  • Playground development

Phase 3

  • Expansion of primary section
  • School library
  • Computer laboratory
  • Multipurpose hall

8. Expected Impact

  • Improved access to quality education
  • Spiritual growth among learners
  • Employment opportunities for local residents
  • Community development and poverty reduction
  • Increased literacy and leadership development

10. Sustainability

The project will become self-sustaining through:

  • School fees
  • Church support
  • Community partnerships
  • Sponsorship programs
  • Income-generating projects developed on the property

11. Partnership Appeal

We respectfully invite churches, organizations, businesses, and individuals in Uganda and the diaspora to partner with us through:

  • Financial contributions
  • Sponsorship of classrooms
  • Educational materials
  • Construction support
  • Technical expertise

Every contribution, regardless of size, will help transform lives and build a brighter future for the children of our community.
