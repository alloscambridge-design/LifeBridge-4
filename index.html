Welcome to LifeBridge Church

LifeBridge Church Uganda is a Christ-centered ministry dedicated to winning souls, making disciples, planting churches and transforming communities through the Gospel of Jesus Christ.

Our Vision

Soul Winning and Church Planting

We exist to reach people with the love of Christ, raise disciples, establish healthy churches and impact nations for God's Kingdom.

Our Story

LifeBridge Church Uganda was founded by Pastor Godfrey Mukwaya in 2022 in the small village of Kajjansi, Wakiso District, Uganda.

Through God's grace the church has grown into a thriving ministry with over 100 registered members.

The ministry continues to grow through evangelism, discipleship, church planting and community outreach.

Founder & Lead Pastor

Pastor Godfrey Mukwaya is the founder and lead pastor of LifeBridge Church Uganda. His passion is to see lives transformed through the Gospel, raise leaders and establish churches that impact communities.

Community Impact & Education Support

LifeBridge Church believes every child deserves hope and access to education.

We support vulnerable children to return to school and complete their education.

Currently the church supports 10 children through educational assistance and mentorship.

We welcome individuals, churches and organizations to partner with us in giving children hope through education and the love of Jesus Christ.
